BIO

Born and raised in Querétaro, Mexico. Luis F. Noguez focuses on the art of production and mixing intending to bring people’s music to life and effectively convey emotion and soul through sonics. Noguez currently lives in Toronto where he has worked with promising emerging artists from different genres such as rock, pop, folk, hip hop, and more.

Noguez's passion for mixing comes from appreciating the high quality of classic records such as Dark Side of the Moon and The Wall by Pink Floyd. His ultimate goal is to deliver records that can match or even surpass the audio quality of the greats.

His skills and abilities have been carefully enhanced by multiple mentors he’s had like Juno Award winner Quique Escamilla and Audio Engineer prodigy Johnny Mak. Of course, his journey could not be made without his cat’s support.
